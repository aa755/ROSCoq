Welcome to the online reference for our ITP2015 submission "ROSCoq : Robots powered by Constructive Reals"


This page guides the reader to key parts of our development:
  • core.v : defintion of time (Time, QTime), continuous functions over time (TContR), derivatives w.r.t. time (isDerivativeOf and many lemmas about bounds on functions based on bounds on their derivatives at rational values of time (e.g. ).
  • CartCR.v : conversion between cartesian and polar coordinates, proofs of correctness of the conversion
  • MsgHandler.v : definiton of a message handler with state, lemmas characterizing its state and output after a handling a finite sequence of mesages
  • roscore.v : definition of topics, messages and software agents
  • ROSCyberPhysicalSystem.v : definition of events, causal ordering, semantics of agents, derivation of semantics of a software agent from its Coq program (SwSemantics), definition and correctness proofs of utility functions which find latest payload on a topic
  • MsgHandler.v : specialization of the above to scpecify the iCreate CPS discussed in the paper. It incudes a defintion of its physical model, specification of its behaviour, and Coq program in the running example of the paper.
